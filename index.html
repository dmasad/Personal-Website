David P. Masad


I am a Ph.D. student at George Mason University's Department of Computational Social Science, interested in applying rigorous quantitative techniques to complex social science questions. I use computational models, network analysis, statistics, and natural language processing to facilitate rapid analysis with an eye towards policy.

My research interests tend to focus on international relations, with an emphasis on national security.

My non-academic interests include cooking, road-tripping, rock climbing, and reading and writing fiction.
